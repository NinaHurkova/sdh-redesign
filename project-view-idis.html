IDIS

Telehealth system for remote patient diagnostics

120K+ Diagnostic results monthly
18 Connected medical devices

Partner's Profile

Background

Ukrainian companies Medstar Solutions LLC and NMT LLC have joined forces to expand digital health opportunities for residents of rural and remote areas. Several brands represent the telemetry system and cloud service IDIS® on the Internet of Medical Things (IoMT) market and successfully implemented them in Ukraine, Brazil, and Austria healthcare systems.

Objective

To build a complex toolset system that will allow a remote initial diagnostic procedure of a patient in the rural health services posts, ambulatory centers, and directly at the patient's home.

Solution

We reached the project's main objective and put our efforts into advancing healthcare services in rural areas. The IDIS® telemedicine diagnostic system is a cloud IoT AI-driven solution for remote patient diagnostics that collects, transmits and interprets patient vital signs data from a distance.

The software gathers the diagnosis data from a portable medical station in one place that is accessible to various healthcare professionals. The internet connection in the system allows to transfer of any medical information, access a patient's EHR, and perform telemedicine conferencing among the health personnel involved in the treatment process.

Telemedicine technology

The video conferencing option simplifies the consulting among healthcare providers and allows them to provide qualified primary care services in rural areas.

Smart connectivity

Using Wi-Fi, 3G, and LTE networks, a patient's diagnostic data could be entered into a personal electronic health record (EHR) and transferred into any medical information system (MIS) or the centralized medical data archive.

Remote health monitoring

The measurements of vital signs recorded in the system allow monitoring of a patient's condition or during rehabilitation remotely.

Diagnostic procedures performance

The system proceeds different procedures to help to determine a diagnosis. For instance, blood pressure measurement, ECG, blood sugar level measurement, urine tests, peak expiratory flow measurement, pulse oximetry, body temperature measurement, etc.

Data protectition

IoT-cloud storage for gathering and transferring clinical healthcare data outside traditional healthcare settings.

Reports and Analysis dashboard

The specialist receives a detailed analysis of the test results, suspected pathology of the patient and other information. AI for doctor decision support.

Technologies

Backend

Java, Firebase, Android SDK, Python, Django, Django REST Framework, SDK for medical Bluetooth devices

Frontend

JavaScript, jQuery, HTML5

Integrations

PostgreSQL, Celery
