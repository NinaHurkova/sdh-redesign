Learning Management System

The learning management platform (LMS) for teaching children English phonetics, word creation, and reading

257K Students
14K Teachers
2.5K Schools

Partner's Profile

Background

Our client is a US-based teacher education R&D center that provides educators with the tools and knowledge to teach reading. Their goal is to prevent and correct decoding deficits in students in all grades. They have created innovative research-based approaches to teaching reading. With interactive, gamified, AI-driven, and multi-sensory digital tools, they teach over 300,000 students in the US and abroad.

Objective

To develop a platform that allows students to improve knowledge of the English language, especially phonetic skills. Build a system that helps Teachers give all necessary information and curricular programs.

Solution

Our team developed the end-to-end AI-driven cloud application focused on preventing and remediating decoding weaknesses in students in all grades (and even adults). We provide educators with the tools and knowledge to teach all students (not just those who learn easily) to read. We make assessments and grouping practical, efficient, and accurate. Our approach to reading instruction is research-based, interactive, explicit, structured, and multisensory. In our lessons, students not only learn to read but enjoy the intelligent learning process.

Rostering (Clever & Classlink)

Teachers and Managers can securely share classes, teachers, students to any other system. Teachers and administrators can save massive amounts of time and avoid countless issues using Rostering. With accurate rosters, publishers prevent missing student accounts and incorrect class groups, which are major stumbling blocks to using online resources in the classroom.

School dashboard

Principles of the School can easily manage licenses through the Dashboard. Monitor the progress of the student in the School.

Application for students

Navigation of units and games in the units with the different type of activity that helps students to improve their knowledges of English phonetics and reading.

Teacher dashboard

All the devices connected to the app are labeled with different colors. A device in an energy-efficient mode is marked as green and becomes yellow and orange in a heating mode.

Wi-Fi thermostats management

The Teacher can easily see the progress of the student for the specific Units and games. Can manage units and games for all students and for each of them separately. See the statistics and manage classes and licenses for the class. Ability remove/edit and add student to the class and transfer them between classes.

Content Management System

Custom CMS is designed to create and manage text and audio content, units and games for students.

AI and ML Modeling

AI helps the teacher to provide individualization and intensifying the learning process. The model reflects the student's behavior and their interaction with the system and allows us to predict the test result at the end of the year.

Technologies

Database
