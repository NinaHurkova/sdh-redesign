Background

Medstar Solutions LLC is the biggest digital health provider in Ukraine and one of the active contributors to national healthcare reforms, and a creator of the first cloud-based medical system in Ukraine with a security certificate for medical data protection. Focusing on the basic needs of doctors, patients, and pharmacists, the company implements medical software and digitizes healthcare administration.

Objective

To create a unified document management system for the health facilities, medical laboratories, and patients' shared use. The system automates the physicians' workflow regarding appointment management, patient records and interaction, treatment process, and laboratory tests.

Visit website

Solution

Our team has successfully developed a comprehensive cloud-based application for patients and healthcare providers. This application comprises two distinct admin dashboards equipped for patients and medical staff. To ensure secure access to the dashboard, each user must authenticate their phone number verification. So all the private and confidential healthcare data is securely stored in the Cloud. We have seamlessly synchronized the Medical Information System (MIS) with eHealth, the flagship electronic healthcare system in Ukraine, automating the management of the central medical database.

Electronic Signatures

The system incorporates e-signatures to ensure the authentication of medical staff, prevent fraud, and enable secure signing and printing of all documents.

Healthcare Professional Profile

Registered healthcare providers have access to a profile that allows them to manage patient queues, conduct house calls, review electronic health records (EHR) before visits, issue e-prescriptions, and facilitate communication with regional healthcare providers.

Patient Profile

By registering as a patient, users can access a patient profile to search and select suitable healthcare providers, schedule physician visits online, view their electronic medical records, and communicate with their treating physician.

Telemedicine

The system incorporates a telemedicine module enabling online video consultations and facilitating doctor-patient and doctor-doctor interactions.

All-in-one

The system is designed to manage various levels of medical facilities, from primary care to hospitals, including maternity hospitals. It provides a comprehensive approach to managing patient history and monitoring at any level of healthcare.

Connection to eHealth

The system is integrated with eHealth (Ukrainian national healthcare system), allowing for accurate financial accounting, storage, and processing of all patient medical data in real time and document flow between healthcare facilities.

Integration with Other Systems.

MIS (HIS) can be integrated with pharmacy management systems to simplify the registration of discount drugs, laboratory systems, and PACS systems. MIS (HIS) is also integrated with the IDIS cloud-based remote patient monitoring system.
